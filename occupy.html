Square occupying

Send a message to the Squaratar Facebook page (Squaratar) with the followings:
➡️ Please read RULES before send a message! ⬅️


(*: Obligatory be sent case of occupy.)
  1. Choose a squareplace coord (this is the location of your pixel) for example like your favorite number, next to favorite user or any...*

  2. Choose a color or color code (this is the color of your pixel).*
    Here is a picker that helps you choose color and code, but it doesn't work on phones, Internet Explorer and Safari 😥: .

  3. Write a text of maximum 350 characters (this is the message of your pixel).

  4. One link you want to promote (it is activated by clicking on your pixel).

  5. Attach a picture with a maximum of 32x32 pixels (it appears when the cursor is over your pixel's message). 📵 It is not visible on the phone. 📵
At least points 1 and 2 of the listed points must be sent to the Squaratar Facebook page. After that, the pixel will appear in the table after a maximum of one day.
Have fun!
